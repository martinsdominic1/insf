Since 1943

Our History

Our parish began as a mission serving Portuguese and Mozambican families who had settled in the area, and grew into the first church in South Africa dedicated to Our Lady of Fátima — a story of faith carried across generations.

📖 Read the Full History
1943
Mission Founded
A mission was established to serve Portuguese and Mozambican families in the area.
1945
Foundation Stone Blessed
The Bishop of Johannesburg blessed the foundation stone on 16 May.
1948
Church Inaugurated
The completed church, seating around 300, was inaugurated on 13 November.
